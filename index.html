Our Mission

The Cutie Cat Rescue is dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abandoned, neglected, and stray cats in our community. We provide medical care, nourishment, and, most importantly, endless love, ensuring they are ready for their perfect forever families.

Read More

Our Vision

We envision a future where every cat is valued, protected, and free from suffering. Through community education, robust foster programs, and proactive rescue efforts, we strive for a no-kill community where every feline has a safe place to call home.

Spend time with your chosen cat.

4. Finalize & Welcome

Pay fee, sign the contract, and take your cat home!
